Ingrediants for a Grilled Cheese

  • 2 Slices of Sourdough Bread or White Bread

  • 3 Tablespoon of Butter

  • Slice of American Cheese

Instructions to Cook a Grilled Cheese

Spread 1 tablespoon of butter on one side of each slice of bread. Placing the butter side down, place your slice of American cheese.

Heat up your pan and place 1 tablespoon of butter and let it melt. Place your bread butter side down on the pan and let it cook until golden and the cheese begins to melt. After 1 minute and 15 seconds flip, and cook it on the other side. It should take 2 minutes and 30 seconds to finish cooking your grill cheese.
